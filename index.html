To use:

  1. Drag the Rickroll Bookmarks button into your bookmarks bar
  2. Visit chrome://bookmarks
  3. Click the three dots in the upper right
  4. Click help center and then instantly begin to spam the escape key
  5. You should load into a blank page, at that point stop spamming escape
  6. Click the bookmark

To un-use:

  1. Drag the UnRickroll Bookmarks button into your bookmarks bar
  2. Visit chrome://bookmarks
  3. Click the three dots in the upper right
  4. Click help center and then instantly begin to spam the escape key
  5. You should load into a blank page, at that point stop spamming escape
  6. Click the bookmark
